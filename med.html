Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our treatments, safety protocols, and what to expect.

Are your treatments safe?

Yes, all our treatments are performed by board-certified medical professionals using FDA-approved products and technologies. Your safety is our top priority.

How long do results last?

Results vary by treatment. Botox typically lasts 3-4 months, dermal fillers 6-18 months, and laser treatments provide long-term skin improvement with proper maintenance.

Is there any downtime?

Most of our treatments have minimal to no downtime. You may experience slight redness or swelling that subsides within 24-48 hours. We'll provide specific aftercare instructions.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options and financing through our partner providers to make our treatments accessible to everyone.
