Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions concerning our aesthetic treatments, timelines, and pre-care.

Are the aesthetic treatments safe?

Yes. All clinical procedures are performed in sterile medical rooms by board-certified physicians. We exclusively use premium, CE-marked, and FDA-approved products.

How long do the results typically last?

It depends on the service. Injectable toxin smoothing typically lasts between 3 to 4 months, premium dermal fillers can last 6 to 18 months, and laser rejuvenation results accumulate over years with solid sun-protection care.

Is there any downtime after procedures?

Most aesthetic injectables require little to no downtime. Slight local swelling or pinpoint bruising can occur but generally resolves inside 24 to 48 hours. Laser skin treatments may show sunburn-like redness for 1 to 3 days.

Do I need a consultation before booking?

Yes. We require a comprehensive skin assessment and medical review before administering any treatment to ensure complete safety and optimal natural alignment.
